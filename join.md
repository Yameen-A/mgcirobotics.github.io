# You Should Join if You...

- Are passionate about technology and/or STEM

- Already enjoy building robots or want to learn

- Already enjoy programming or want to learn

- Want to work with the latest robotics technology

- Want to enhance your future portfolio

- Want to compete and have fun with your friends

# How to Join

Robotics Club applications are currently **closed**.

In the meantime, if you're interested in robotics you can check out our [resources](/resources) page to learn independently and/or follow our social media pages at the bottom to be notified the next time our applications are available.
