Who We Are

We're a passionate and dedicated group of students who make up the Robotics Club of Marc Garneau Collegiate Institute, a secondary school located in Flemingdon Park, Toronto

What We Do

Our club aims to educate students and promote robotics, encouraging teamwork through providing hands-on and interactive experiences with robotics such as competing in tournaments

Our Website

This website is host to information about us, how to join, our past achievements, our contact information, and a wealth of resources related to robotics
